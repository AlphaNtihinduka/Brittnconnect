RELIABLE CONNECTIVITY SOLUTIONS FOR AFRICA

Innovative broadband technologies and cost-effective solutions are critical to ensure universal, fast internet access. Our state-of-the-art Wireless and satellite broadband infrastructure enables us to offer reliable internet access all over East Africa, to populations, businesses and the public sector, instantly and at affordable prices. Brittn connect supports various IP services like internet/intranet

access, VoIP and multicasting. Its high performance offers IP broadband services in a cost effective way over Lte, Ku- and Ka-band platforms. It is perfectly suited for servicing home users, Small Office and Home Office (SOHO), Small and Medium Enterprises (SME) as well as supporting applications like telemetry networks, Point Of Sale (POS) or banking. For every Brittn connect Link, the equipment consists of an Outdoor integrated antenna unit, Modem/ Satellite Router, cabling and a wireless device.
